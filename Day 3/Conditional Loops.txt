Conditional Loops

Introduction to loops

Loops are used to execute a group of statements repeatedly

Syntax:
        For ((exp1; exp2; exp3))
        Do
                Statement1
                Statement2
                Statement3
        Done

Syntax:
        For variable in sequence/range
        Do
                Statement1
                Statement2
                Statement3
        Done

Syntax:
While [ condition ]
        Do
                Statement1
                Statement2
                Statement3
        Done
